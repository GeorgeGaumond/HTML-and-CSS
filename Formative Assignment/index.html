Hacking is on the Rise

There are way more cases of hacking taking place these days. Computers are more ubiquitous than ever, with almost half of the world owning a computer and many with a fast internet connection. Coding is popular with many students, and many like to challenge institutions they disagree with or have not yet invaded.

An example of a hacking attack that had an extremely negative impact on a target was the Citibank hack in 1995. Vladimir Levin, a programmer for Russia, stole the private details of customers by recording the phone calls they have with the bank. He managed to steal around $10,000,000. Even after he was prosecuted, the government could not retrieve all the money he stole.





















The services we provide

No website is 100% secure. A hacker has numerous methods to determine how your website is insecure and slip right in in without you aware. Some hackers sell the information they've stolen to other criminals, whereas others just love to break their way into places they shouldn't be. No mater what drives them into breaking in, these people are a threat. We have an extremely professional team with decades of experience overall. They have protected various large companies, banks and governmental agencies from whoever is breaking in, for whatever reason.

The first thing we shall do is to check every line of code in your website. Some mistakes are easy to spot, such as passwords stored in JavaScript files, other mistakes are more difficult to spot at first. As soon as we suspect that a hacker, worker or anyone is breaking in we shall report it imediately. We will block the potential hackers and also try to identify who is responsible so they can be reported to authorities.
